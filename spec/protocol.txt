AutoItRPC protocol specification


RPC Messsage
 -------------------------------
| message_length | FS | message |
 -------------------------------

message_length
/^[1-9][0-9]+$/

Max: MAX_STRINGLEN

message
 ---------------------
| command | GS | args |
 ---------------------


args
 -------
| array |
 -------


Array Format (arr)
 ---------------------------------------------
| STX | value | RS | value | RS | value | ETX |
 ---------------------------------------------

 Value Format
 ------------------
| type | US | data | 
 ------------------


Supported Types
i32
i64
num
str
bool
hwnd
arr (STX)


e.g.
["blah", 24, false]

 ----------------------------------------------------------------------
| str | STX | blah | ETX | i32 | STX | 24 | ETX | bool | 1 | ETX | ETX |
 ----------------------------------------------------------------------

Example Command Message
 --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 42 | FS | MouseClick | GS | STX | str | US | left | RS | i32 | US | 150 | RS | int | US | 225 | RS | i32 | US | 50 | ETX |
 --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Translates to
MouseClick("left", 150, 225, 50)


Result Format
 ----------------------------
| result_length | FS | value |
 ----------------------------

result_length
/^[1-9][0-9]+$/

Max: MAX_STRINGLEN



 Example Result From WinList()

 -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
| 50 | FS | STX | STX | i32 | US | 1 | RS | str | ETX | STX | str | US | Chrome | RS | hwnd | US | 0xDEADBEEF | ETX | ETX |
 -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

 Translates to
 [[1, ""], ["Chrome", "0xDEADBEEF"]]
